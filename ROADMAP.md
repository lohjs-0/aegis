## Contributing to the Roadmap

Have a feature idea? Open an [issue](https://github.com/lohjs-0/aegis/issues) with the label `roadmap` and describe:

```
- What the feature is
- Why it fits ÆGIS's philosophy
- How you'd expect it to work
- Whether you'd be willing to build it
```

The most impactful contributions are those that increase pressure, realism, and learning depth — in that order.
